# Tier A sequence — your best prospects

High-fit, established, can afford you. Worth the most personalization: spend 30
seconds on each one. If you can, open their website and swap the first line for
something specific you actually noticed. The `{{Proof}}` line (real reviews/rating
from the list) makes a great, honest opener.

Merge fields: `{{FirstName}}` `{{Business}}` `{{TypeReadable}}` `{{Hook}}`
`{{Example}}` `{{CalLink}}` `{{YourAddress}}`. See `vertical-snippets.md` for Hook/Example.
If you don't know a first name, use "there" — never guess.
